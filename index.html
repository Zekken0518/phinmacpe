About PHINMA COC

PHINMA Cagayan de Oro College (COC) is committed to providing quality education that prepares students for the demands of the global workforce. Our Computer Engineering program is designed to develop skilled professionals who can innovate and lead in the tech industry.

Excellence in Education

Our program is accredited and recognized for its high standards and quality curriculum designed by industry experts.

Cutting-Edge Facilities

Access state-of-the-art laboratories, equipment, and software to gain hands-on experience with the latest technologies.

Industry Connections

Benefit from our strong partnerships with tech companies for internships, job placements, and collaborative projects.

Data Engineer
